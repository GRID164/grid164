How to get it

What you get
when you invest in GRID164

GRID164 is sold only as digital files for 3D printing — we don't sell ready-made physical pieces or an assembled track. When you buy, you receive the STL files for every piece, the printing manual and the assembly manual/video.

⚡ Instant digital delivery
3D Printing Files
Digital · No shipping · Instant delivery

What you buy are the STL files for all GRID164 pieces, ready to send straight to your 3D printer — plus the printing manual and the assembly manual/video. There's no shipping of physical pieces: you (or a 3D printing service near you) print and build your own circuit.

  • STL files for every piece— instant digital delivery as soon as you purchase through the download link
  • Printing manual included— recommended material and ideal 3D printer settings
  • Assembly manual and video included— step by step showing how to build each piece and structure the circuit (sent by email)
  • No shipping cost for pieces— being a 100% digital product, you save the cost you'd otherwise pay to ship physical pieces
  • Available in every country— anyone anywhere in the world can buy it, payment permitting
  • Unlimited pieces— print as many as you want to expand your circuit whenever you like
Don't have a 3D printer? No problem! You don't need to own one: there are 3D printing services in practically every city that will print the files for you at an affordable price. A quick search for "3D printing" in your area will do the trick.
🌎 We only sell the files, not the physical track — this significantly lowers your final cost, since there's no shipping of heavy, bulky pieces: the files arrive by email or download link anywhere in the world.
🔩 Final assembly of the circuit also uses easy-to-find complementary materials, such as PVC channels to structure the track's edges and guides — a common item at electrical supply stores and easy to find online too. It's all explained in the assembly manual.
Why not design and print the pieces yourself? Developing the fit, testing print tolerances and adjusting every module from scratch is a long process full of trial and error. By buying the GRID164 files you skip that step: these are modules already designed and tested, ready to print and assemble without rework.
